## Beautiful maps, any city

Type a city name, pick a style, get a poster. Built on OpenStreetMap data with a dozen visual themes ranging from blueprint and Japanese ink to neon cyberpunk.

Cities covered include San Francisco, Barcelona, Venice, Tokyo, Mumbai, Marrakech, Singapore, Dubai, and any other location with OSM coverage.

### Themes

blueprint · sunset · warm_beige · japanese_ink · contrast_zones · terracotta · neon_cyberpunk · forest · midnight_blue · and more

### Stack

Python · OpenStreetMap / OSMnx · Matplotlib · GeoPandas
